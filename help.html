Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add my income? – Navigate to the Budget page and enter your income details.
  • How do I add an expense? – Go to the Expenses page, choose a category, and add your expense.
  • How do I manage bills? – Visit the Bills page to schedule your bills and track due dates.
  • How do I compare prices? – Use the Compare page to input prices and view a visual comparison.
